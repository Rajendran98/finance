Registrations and licenses

Registration and licenses as required can be obtained:

  • MSME (Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises) registration as under MSMED Act; Udhyog Aadhaar ᴺᴱᵂ
  • Import Export (IE) Code licenses
  • Corporation license- Trade license
  • Shop and establishment act registration – Labour license
  • Professional tax registration
  • FSSAI registration​​​ (​Food Safety and Standards Authority of India)
  • Basic Registration – If the annual turnover of the business is less than 12 lakhs then the food business operator shall get registered with Registration Form A
  • State license – If the annual turnover of the business is between 12 to 20 crores then the food business operator shall apply for State license Form B
  • Central license – If the annual turnover of the business is above 20 crores, then the food business operator shall apply for Central License Form B
  • ISO Certificate

Stock Audits

We undertake stock audits in and around Chennai.

  • Physical verification
  • Valuation of inventories and variance
  • Verification of controls, policies and valuation systems with regard to stock and inventories
  • Verification of documents/ securities and determination of drawing power

LLP

  • Annual filing- Form 11

Digital Signature (DSC token)

Digital signatures are mandatory for filing and tenders. The following classes of USB e-tokens are available:

  • Class 2- For IT, GST and MCA filing
  • Class 3- For Government e-tenders
  • DGFT digital signature- For EXIM organisations

Trusts

Various registrations and certificates in relation to charitable trusts:

  • Formation of the trust and trust deed
  • Registration for 80 G exemption certificate
  • Registration under section 12 AA for exempting receipts from taxation
  • Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act, 2010 compliance​

Net worth certificates

BPO

Claims processing for MNCs

For most companies, the management of travel and entertainment expenses is not a core competency. We help in streamlining and managing the processing of claims in an organisation and help the organisation and management focus on core competencies. The firm has over 15 years of experience in handling large MNC’s claims processing pan India. This reduces cost for the organisation in relation to processing of claims and also reduces the time delay in the process.

Travel and field force expenses audit

We assist in verification of travel and field force expense of employees in relation to policy guidelines of the company on a pan India basis. The firm has over 8 years of experience in handling such audits.

Payroll management

We undertake outsourced payroll management for organisations of all sizes simultaneously complying with the various regulations with regard to PF, ESI and TDS in respect of salary for employees.
